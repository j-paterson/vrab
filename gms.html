GENERAL MEETINGS
First General Meeting

Are you curious and/or enthusiastic about Virtual Reality? Come try out demos on the HTC Vive, Oculus Rift, and Google Cardboard platforms and get to meet other students who are excited about VR! This meeting will also be our first Boot Camp talk about Product, Design, and Business in VR. Learn about the current state of the VR industry and the design considerations involved in making immersive experiences.

Tilt Brush Pictionary

Tilt Brush lets you paint in 3D, literally bringing a new dimension to traditional painting. People will try to get others to guess what they're painting and try to get as many points as they can in a set amount of time.

How to Give a Killer Virtual Reality Demo

Teaching volunteers for the Virtual Experience Convention how to give a killer demo.

Designing for Motion Sickness

In Spring 2016 Six Flags revealed a VR rollercoaster: The New Revolution. The thought that immediately comes to mind is: "Won't people get sick?" Come find out how VR developers have dealt with the potential for motion sickness and how this impacts VR design moving forward!
